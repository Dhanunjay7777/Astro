What is Astrology?

Astrology is the study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies, such as planets and stars, and their influence on human affairs and natural phenomena.

Zodiac Signs

The zodiac is divided into twelve signs, each associated with specific personality traits and characteristics. Your zodiac sign is determined by your birthdate.

Horoscopes

Horoscopes provide insights into your future based on your zodiac sign. They are often used for guidance and entertainment.

Astrological Elements

Astrology is associated with four elements: Fire, Earth, Air, and Water. Each element represents specific qualities and signs.

Astrology and Relationships

Many people turn to astrology to gain insights into their compatibility with others. Learn about astrological compatibility.

Astrological Houses

Astrological houses represent areas of your life, such as career, relationships, and family. Explore the meaning of each house.
